Peanut Sauce Recipe
1/2 cup smooth natural peanut butter
1/4 cup soy Sauce
1 tbsp rice vinegar
1 tbsp maple syrup
1 tbsp sameul oblek
1 tbsp fresh ginger, minced
1-2 cloves garlic, minced
1/3 to 1/4 cup water, as needed

Mix all ingredients together untill well encorporated. Add water as need to thin out sauce.
